How to play

Click a piece above — the dots show every square it can move to, the teal rings show captures. It works exactly the same on the real board.

The goal

Forget capturing everything — the point is to trap the enemy King. When a King is attacked it's in check; if it can't escape, that's checkmate and the game ends. No legal move but not in check is a stalemate — a draw, and the pot is split.

Your turn

White moves first, then you alternate. Click your piece, the dots appear, click a dot. That's it. Hit Undo to take a move back, Flip to spin the board.

Special moves (handled for you)

Castling — the King and a Rook move together to tuck the King somewhere safe. Promotion — get a pawn to the far side and it becomes a Queen. En passant — a rare pawn capture; just trust the dots.

◆ Diamonds

Your single wallet across every game in the house — chess, spades, whatever comes next. Earn them by winning and from your daily check-in.

The wager

Both players ante Diamonds into escrow — that's the pot. Win by checkmate, resignation, or running their clock out, and you take the whole pot. Draw and antes come back.

Levels & daily

Every game earns XP toward your next level, unlocking titles, frames, and modes. Check in daily for a Diamond bonus that grows with your streak.

Hints & the Purist bonus

New? Leave Hints on — we'll suggest a strong move in teal. As you improve, switch them off: win a match completely hint-free and earn a Purist bonus on top of the pot. Skill pays more.
